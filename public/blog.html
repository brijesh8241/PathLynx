Recruitment Loop

The Standard Pipeline

1. Sourcing & Application

On-Campus: Direct university visits.
Off-Campus: Career portals and LinkedIn. Referrals are key—they often guarantee a human eye on your resume.

2. Online Assessment (OA)

Automated coding challenge (60-90 mins). Typically 2-4 algorithmic problems. Tests raw speed and DS familiarity.

3. Technical Interviews

DSA Focus: Live coding on whiteboard/shared editor. Evaluates thought process, communication, and optimization skills.

4. Behavioral / HR Round

Evaluates cultural fit using the STAR method. Focuses on leadership, conflict resolution, and long-term vision.

5. The Offer

Review by the hiring committee follows. Successful candidates receive a package including Base, Bonus, and RSUs.
