🧠 Core Algorithms

  • DFS/BFS: Essential for Graph problems. Used in 30%+ of Tier-1 tech interviews.
  • Sliding Window: Converts O(N^2) subarray problems into linear O(N) time complexity.
  • Dynamic Programming: Break down complex optimizations using memoization.

⭐ The STAR Method

Structure your stories to maintain clarity and impact:

Situation: Describe the specific context.
Task: What was your specific responsibility?
Action: Detailed steps YOU took to solve it.
Result: Outcomes backed by quantifiable metrics.

🔥 High-Frequency Questions

  • "Tell me about a time you failed."
  • "Describe a conflict with a teammate."
  • "Why do you want to join our team?"
